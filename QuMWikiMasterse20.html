3.5 First (Demo) Report

Open RStudio, click onto the triangle looking down, next to the “new script”-icon, choose “R Markdown…”.


The ”Install Required Packages” massage occurs, which is recommend to confirm (“Yes”).


Type in your Title and the name of the author (can be changed later) and choose the “PDF”-option. Save your report.


Click onto the triangle looking down, next to the “Knit”-icon, choose “Knit to PDF”. The knitting process may take a bit of time. After the “STOP”-icon disappeared, you should see a beatiful report in front of you, which is saved in the same folder as your RMarkdown script-file.
