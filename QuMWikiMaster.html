Welcome to the Wiki of Quantitative Methods!

As you will learn R in the lecture, this Wiki will be helpful to go through the content in your own speed and more detail. For every topic in the course there is one session in the Wiki prepared for you. It augments the content of the lecture, gives examples and handling tips for R. You are supposed to have a look at each sssion before and after the lecture.

A glossary is also available containing useful R-commands and explanations for other terms. Concerning R-commands do not forget to concern the R-help for more details or the “true meaning” of the command.

This Wiki does not contain every detail on R, but provides a good base of knowledge. It will help you to understand the lectures, do your homework as well as your case studies, but the Wiki does not claim to be exhaustive.

Please report mistakes and ambiguities to fscheuer@wu.ac.at.
